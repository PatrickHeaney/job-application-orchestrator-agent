# /help

Guide to Career Orchestration commands.

## Setup
- `/set-root`: Establish your sovereignty boundary.
- `/sync-ledger`: Refresh the central application dashboard.
- `/scaffold-profile`: Verify/Refine your master UVP.

## Value Stream
- `/new-application <folder>`: Launch a new job lead.
- `/orchestrate-application <folder>`: Start interactive 9-step workflow.
- `/analyze-job <folder>`: Identify pivot points & narrative strategy.
- `/draft-resume <folder>`: Generate tailored resume.
- `/draft-cover-letter <folder>`: Generate strategic proposal.

## Interview & Follow-up
- `/prep-interview <folder>`: Generate Q&A speaker notes.
- `/check-gmail`: Scan for recruiter communications.
